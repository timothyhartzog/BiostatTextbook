About This Textbook

This open-access interactive textbook was designed for students in health and life science programs who need a solid grounding in biostatistical methods. Each chapter combines:

  • Clear conceptual explanations with real-world health examples
  • Mathematical foundations and key formulas
  • Interactive tools so you can explore concepts with your own data
  • Self-assessment quizzes with immediate feedback

No installation is required — everything runs in your browser.
